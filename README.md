## 🖌 Overview  

Logic Pro Professional Suite is a complete music production environment that serves the full range of professional audio work from initial composition and arrangement through tracking, mixing, mastering, and delivery in a single deeply integrated application.

Session Players in Logic Pro Professional Suite provide AI-generated backing performances on drums, bass, and keyboard that adapt to your song's chord structure and style, providing professional-sounding foundation tracks without session musicians.

Spatial Audio mixing tools with Dolby Atmos support produce immersive three-dimensional mixes that render correctly on Apple Music and spatial audio playback systems, making professional Atmos production accessible within the same DAW environment.

Flex Time and Flex Pitch editing provides surgical timing and pitch correction for recorded audio at the individual note and transient level, keeping performances perfectly in time and tune without the artifacts of less sophisticated correction methods.

Logic Pro Professional Suite is the music production platform that Apple musicians, composers, and producers choose for its combination of professional depth, exceptional value, and the hardware integration that makes every aspect of production feel native and optimized.
